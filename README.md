## 🗺️ 6-Week Study Plan to Land the Job

### Week 1: SQL & Excel Mastery
- **Files:** 01, 02, 03, 04, 05, 06
- **Goal:** Write complex queries without looking them up. Build a pivot table in under 2 minutes.
- **Practice:** Solve 5 SQL problems daily on LeetCode or HackerRank.

### Week 2: BI, Statistics & Python
- **Files:** 07, 08, 09, 10
- **Goal:** Build a dashboard in Power BI/Tableau. Run a t-test and interpret p-values. Clean a messy dataset with pandas.
- **Practice:** Re-create a dashboard from a dataset you find online.

### Week 3: Architecture & Engineering Fundamentals
- **Files:** 16, 17, 18, 19
- **Goal:** Explain ETL vs ELT, design a star schema, compare AWS vs GCP, understand SCD Type 2.
- **Practice:** Draw an ER diagram for an e-commerce site. Explain it out loud.

### Week 4: Soft Skills, Governance & Process
- **Files:** 14, 15, 20, 21, 22, 23, 24
- **Goal:** Tell a data story in 3 minutes. Explain GDPR in plain English. Walk through a Git workflow.
- **Practice:** Record yourself explaining a project to a non-technical friend.

### Week 5: Build Your Portfolio
- **File:** 27
- **Goal:** Complete 2 end-to-end projects with GitHub repos, READMEs, and live dashboards.
- **Recommended:** Customer Churn Analysis + Sales Performance Dashboard.
- **Deploy:** Tableau Public + GitHub + Medium blog post.

### Week 6: Interview Prep & Negotiation
- **Files:** 11, 12, 13, 25, 26
- **Goal:** Answer behavioral questions with STAR method. Solve puzzles under pressure. Know your walk-away salary number.
- **Practice:** Mock interview with a friend. Record and review.

## 🎯 How to Use Each File

### For Technical Interviews
1. Read the **Theoretical** questions first to build vocabulary.
2. Study the **Scenario-Based** questions to see how concepts apply to real problems.
3. Practice explaining answers out loud — interviews are verbal, not written.

### For Portfolio Building
- Use **File 27** to pick projects that match your target industry.
- Use **File 24** (Dashboard Design) to make your visualizations look professional.
- Use **File 21** (Git) to ensure your repos are clean and well-documented.

### For Salary Negotiation
- Read **File 26** *before* you get an offer. Know your BATNA and walk-away number.
- Research market rates using the tools listed in Q4.
- Practice your negotiation script out loud.

### For Behavioral Interviews
- Use **File 11** to prepare 5-7 STAR stories from your experience.
- Use **File 14** to learn how to communicate technical findings to non-technical stakeholders.

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Don't memorize — understand.** Interviewers care more about your reasoning than rote answers.
2. **Quantify everything.** "I improved reporting" → "I reduced reporting time by 20 hours/week."
3. **Show your work.** Push code to GitHub. Share dashboards on Tableau Public. Write about your projects.
4. **Practice out loud.** Reading silently is not the same as explaining verbally under pressure.
5. **Know your stories.** Have 3-5 STAR stories ready: a time you failed, a time you led, a time you solved a hard problem.
6. **Ask questions.** The interview is bilateral. Ask about the data stack, team structure, and growth opportunities.
7. **Negotiate.** Almost every initial offer has room. Use File 26. The worst they can say is no.
